About Odyssey

The Beginning
of Odyssey.

Every great mission starts with curiosity. Engineers, innovators and explorers unite to build intelligent rovers and aerial systems capable of tackling the challenges of planetary exploration.

01 — Who We Are

Where innovation meets exploration.

Odyssey is the official Rovers & Drones Team of K. J. Somaiya School of Engineering. We design and build intelligent rovers, drones and autonomous systems — engineering machines that compete, innovate and push the frontier of what students can create.

02 — Our Mission

Turning ideas into impact.

To inspire and empower students to transform ideas into impactful engineering solutions. Through collaboration, research and hands-on learning, Odyssey builds technologies that push the boundaries of exploration — while developing the future leaders of engineering.

03 — Our Vision

Reliable, intelligent, real-world systems.

We aim to build innovative and intelligent autonomous systems that push the boundaries of robotics, drones and rover technology. Our vision is to create reliable, efficient, real-world solutions by combining cutting-edge engineering with creativity and collaboration.

Through continuous research, hands-on development and participation in national and international competitions, we cultivate technical excellence, inspire innovation, and empower students to solve complex engineering challenges — making a meaningful impact on the future of autonomous technology.
